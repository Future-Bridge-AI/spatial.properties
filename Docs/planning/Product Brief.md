3. **Distribute & Cache** ‚Äì Packs are served via a CDN, allowing devices or apps to fetch just the context they need‚Äîfast, offline-capable, and cacheable.
4. **Integrate & Automate** ‚Äì APIs feed Spatial Packs directly into analytics, LLMs, and autonomous systems for place-aware decisioning.
